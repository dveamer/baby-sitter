# Tester Safe Setup Guide

부모/테스터에게 바로 전달할 수 있는 영어 기준 안내문 초안입니다. 현지 번역 전에 이 내용을 기준본으로 쓰는 편이 좋습니다.

## Baby Sitter quick setup

1. Prepare one Android phone for the nursery.
2. Place the phone on a stable stand outside the crib.
3. Connect the nursery phone and the parent phone to the same home Wi-Fi.
4. Open Baby Sitter on the nursery phone and follow the tutorial.
5. Turn on the sensing features you want to test.
6. Connect from the parent device browser using the QR flow.
7. If you want, record a lullaby in a parent's voice before the first night.

## Safety first

* Keep the phone, stand, and charging cable outside the crib and out of reach.
* Do not place the phone inside the crib or on the crib rail.
* Use a stable surface or stand that will not tip over.
* Make sure cords do not hang into the sleep area.
* Keep using normal caregiver judgment during naps and at night.

## What this app is for

* Checking the nursery from another room on the same Wi-Fi
* Noticing sound and motion before a full wake-up
* Playing a lullaby in a parent's voice
* Reviewing saved moments around wake-ups

## What this app is not for

* It is not a medical device.
* It is not an emergency alert guarantee.
* It does not replace caregiver supervision.
* It is not designed for remote viewing outside the home Wi-Fi.
